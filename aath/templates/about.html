About Us

Meet Your Trusted Mental Health Experts

At Aathmajythi Psychiatric Counseling Services, we bring over 25 years of expert care to Mysore and Bangalore. Our seasoned team of professionals specializes in treating anxiety disorders, phobias, depression, and more. We offer personalized support through individual counseling, family therapy, couple counseling, marriage therapy, and child guidance.

Our mission is to provide compassionate, effective counseling that empowers individuals and families to thrive. Trust in our experience and commitment to your mental health journey. Discover a path to well-being with Aathmajythi, your partners in mental health excellence.
